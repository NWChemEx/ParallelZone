template<typename T>
struct parallelzone::mpi_helpers::NeedsSerialized< std::vector< T >, std::enable_if_t< std::is_trivially_copyable_v< T > > >

Specialization for std::vector<T> (T trivially copyable)

Trivially copyable types are stored contiguously, thus a std::vector of them does not need to be serialized (it's perhaps worth noting that std::vector itself is not trivially copyable).
